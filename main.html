Munyakazi Chicken Farm is a thriving poultry farm located in Musanze, Rwanda. Established with a vision to revolutionize poultry farming in the region, Munyakazi Chicken Farm focuses on producing high-quality day-old chicks and providing comprehensive support to local farmers. The farm is known for its commitment to biosecurity measures, ensuring that the chickens are raised in a clean and healthy environment. Munyakazi Chicken.
